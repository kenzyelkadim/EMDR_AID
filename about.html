About EMDR

This web-based tool is designed to support therapists during sessions by providing a simple, guided visual stimulus for bilateral eye movement. It helps maintain consistent pacing through smooth, controlled motion, reducing the need for manual guidance and allowing therapists to stay focused on the client.

The interface is intentionally minimal and responsive, making it easy to use in real time during sessions. Adjustable speed and straightforward controls allow the stimulus to be tailored to individual needs without adding distraction or complexity.

This project was built with a focus on usability and clarity, aiming to create a practical tool that integrates naturally into therapeutic workflows. It is intended as a supportive aid for trained professionals and does not replace clinical judgment or formal therapeutic training.
